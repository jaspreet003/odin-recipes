Easy Tomato Twirl Pasta

Description

This quick and flavorful tomato pasta is a perfect meal for any day of the week. With a rich tomato sauce, fresh basil, and a hint of Parmesan, it's a simple dish that's sure to please everyone.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any kind)
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add minced garlic, and saut√© for about 1 minute.
  3. Add diced tomatoes with juice, salt, and pepper to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
