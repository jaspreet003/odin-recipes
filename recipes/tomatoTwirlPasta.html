Tomato Twirl Pasta

Description

Coated in a blend of brown sugar and butter, glazed carrots make a flexible accompaniment that pairs well with everything from a casual weeknight meatloaf to a festive Sunday ham or the Thanksgiving turkey. These caramelized delights are sure to win over the kids, while their sophisticated flavor also garners admiration from the adults!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds carrots, peeled and cut into sticks
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground white pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Put the carrots in a big saucepan.

    Step 2

    Add water until it's 1 inch deep.

    Step 3

    Heat until the water boils.

    Step 4

    Lower the heat, cover the pan, and let the carrots simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until they are soft.

    Step 5

    Drain the water and put the carrots in a bowl.

    Step 6

    In the same pan, melt butter over low heat.

    Step 7

    Mix in brown sugar, salt, and white pepper until they dissolve.

    Step 8

    Add the carrots back into the pan.

    Step 9

    Toss the carrots so they're covered in the brown sugar mix.

    Step 10

    Cook and stir for 1 to 3 minutes until the carrots are warm and glazed.
