Hearty Bean Chili

Description

A warm and hearty bean chili that's perfect for any season. Loaded with beans, tomatoes, and spices, this chili is both nutritious and full of flavor. It's an easy-to-make dish that's ideal for feeding a crowd or enjoying as a comforting meal at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) beans (kidney, black, or pinto), drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (28 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water or vegetable broth (optional, for thinner chili)
  • Shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and bell pepper, cooking until softened.
  2. Add beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine.
  3. If desired, add water or vegetable broth to reach your preferred chili consistency.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with shredded cheese, sour cream, and green onions if desired.
